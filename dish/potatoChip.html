Potato chips recipes

Description

Today we gona make a crispy Potato chips

Ingredients

    • 250 gm pasta macaroni
    • 1 yellow bell pepper
    • 2 tablespoon butter
    • 1/2 teaspoon white pepper powder
    • salt as required
    • 4 tablespoon tomato ketchup
    • 1 red bell pepper
    • 2 onion
    • 50 gm cheddar cheese
    • 1 teaspoon chilli powder
    • 1/4 cup corn

Steps

  1. Step 1 Chop all the vegetables and boil the macaroni
  2. Step 2 Tenderize the chopped veggies
  3. Step 3 Cook veggies in spices and mix with boiled macaroni
  4. Step 4 Ready to be served
  5. Step 5 Eat
