Spagheti recipes

Description

Easy Weeknight Spaghetti with Meat Sauce

Ingredients

  • Salt
  • Pasta
  • Spagheti sauce

Steps

  1. Step 1 Use a large pot
  2. Step 2 Load up the pot with lots of water
  3. Step 3 Salt the water
  4. Step 4 Bring the water to a full, rolling boil
  5. Step 5 Stir to keep the pasta from sticking
  6. Step 6 Test the pasta two minutes before it’s “ready”
  7. Step 7 Save a scoop of pasta water
  8. Step 8 Drain, toss with sauce, and serve hot
